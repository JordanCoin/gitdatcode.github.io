$(datcode) is a community made of Black and POC coders
serving as a place of sharing, collaboration, and plain ol' conversation.
We want to make ourselves more visible, for our sake and others.


Because coding is fun; you should get into it.

.share

Sharing of knowledge is a big part of the community. Through our workshops, our Slack group discussions, or simply adding #DatCode to a tweet, there's a place for you to ask questions or share what you know. We're open to beginners and experts alike.

.collaborate

If you think you have a pretty cool idea, let it be known and share it with the community. If other coders think so too, hack it out! Maybe even set up a hackathon, if you really want to up the stakes.

You could find your next project team here, and we want you to. Go forth and code.

.converse

As much as we're the same, we're different. Meet people from your area at our socials, or chop it up amongst the community in our Slack group. Whatever you want to talk about, it's open for discussion.

Join in the community any time you'd like.
Get an invite to our Slack group
or just throw the hashtag #DatCode on your Twitter or Facebook post.

We'll see you soon!
